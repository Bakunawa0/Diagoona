Age-adjusted suicide rate drops by a third globally

Between 1990 and 2016, the total number of deaths from suicide increased by 6.7% globally; however, the age-standardized mortality rate for suicide dropped by 32.7%, according to a systematic analysis of the Global Burden of Disease Study 2016. Read more.

President Trump issues national call to action to end veteran suicide

In March, President Donald J. Trump signed an executive order to establish a Cabinet-level task force focused on empowering veterans and preventing veteran suicide.

Suicidal ideation can occur despite antidepressant use in major depression

About one in five patients with major depressive disorder experienced high or fluctuating suicidal ideation despite receiving antidepressant treatment for 12 weeks, according to results published in Journal of Clinical Psychiatry

VA, DoD update guidelines for patients at risk for suicide

The U.S. Department of Veteran Affairs and the U.S. Department of Defense updated their clinical practice guidelines for screening, evaluation, treatment and management of patients at risk for suicide.

Ketamine improves suicidal ideation in veterans with PTSD, MDD

Veterans with PTSD and major depressive disorder saw significant improvement in suicidal ideation after receiving IV infusions of ketamine over a 12-day period, according to a poster presented at the Anxiety and Depression Association of America annual conference.

Every September, we celebrate the Suicide Prevention Month
Let's make use of what we have and save those who need us. September 10 is Suicide Prevention Day.
Hold on before it's too late. Stay strong!
Depression can be like being buried alive - your true self being underneath all the numbness and fake facial expressions to show that you are fine when you are not.
The hands of depression grip tightly, and like an iron fist it smashes your resolve and will to live even when all are fine.
"Despair is a cruel companion - it robs you of everything, especially the choices that still lie within your control." - Brownell Landrum
Never be in sorrow, for your family and friends will always be there to support you, and will be grief-stricken when you are hurt - or worse, gone.
Although sometimes we "disconnect", at the end of the day, it is always comforting to know that there is a light shining in the darkness. You can be that light to those around you, just as they can be the light to you.
